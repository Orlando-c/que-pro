  • Can you describe what makes a good or bad password and an additional step for authentication?

  • Good passwords are complex, using a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols, making it more difficult for a brute force attack. Two-factor authentication is also a helpful method, which involves verifying a login from an external source, such as through email or a code.

  • Can you explain the difference between Symmetric and Asymmetric encryption?

  • Symmetric encryption uses a single key for both encryption and decryption, while asymmetric encryption uses two keys, a public key for encryption and a private key for decryption.
