HIPA

HIPA is involved in the analysis of a prompt. HIPA stands for

  1. Historical Context
  2. Intended Audience
  3. Purpose of the document
  4. Authors PoV
    • Gender
    • Education
    • Nationality
    • Age
    • Profession
    • Religion
    • Social Status
    • Race/Ethnicity

This is very important because this can help you understand where the prompt is coming from and to better answer the question. For example...

The purpose of this document is to convey the... The intended audience of the document is to the... The historical context of the document is... The Authors POV includes his...

PEDITs

PEDIT stands for

  1. People
  2. Events
  3. Dates
  4. Ideas
  5. Things

PEDITS are used as evidence or details to support your topic sentance. For example
